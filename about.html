Our story

Dallas's Premier Carpentry Team

To provide exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service that transforms homes and exceeds expectations.

Garcia Carpentry was founded in 2008 by master carpenter Miguel Garcia, who brought over a decade of experience from high-end custom homes. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, Miguel built a reputation for precision and reliability. Over the years, the team grew, but the core values remained: use the best materials, respect the client's home, and deliver on time.

Today, Garcia Carpentry serves homeowners across Dallas with a full range of carpentry services. We take pride in our work, from intricate crown molding to expansive decks. Every project is a partnership with our clients, ensuring the final result reflects their vision and our craftsmanship.
