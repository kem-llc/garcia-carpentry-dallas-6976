Our story

Crafting Quality Woodwork in Dallas Since 2005

Our mission is to provide Dallas homeowners with exceptional carpentry that combines timeless craftsmanship with modern precision. We believe every home deserves beauty and functionality, and we deliver both with integrity and care.

Garcia Carpentry was founded by master carpenter Carlos Garcia, who brought over a decade of experience from high-end custom home building to create a dedicated carpentry service in Dallas. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of skilled artisans, all sharing a passion for wood and a commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of transforming hundreds of homes across Dallas, from historic bungalows in Lakewood to modern estates in Highland Park. Every project, whether a simple trim installation or an elaborate custom library, receives the same meticulous attention to detail and personal touch. We take pride in our reputation for reliability, cleanliness, and superior workmanship.
