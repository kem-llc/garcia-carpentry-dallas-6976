Our story

Crafted with Pride in Dallas, TX

Garcia Carpentry is dedicated to providing exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service to homeowners in Dallas. We believe in building relationships as strong as our joinery.

Garcia Carpentry was founded by master carpenter Luis Garcia, who started his career as an apprentice in a small shop in Dallas. For over 15 years, he honed his skills in custom furniture, trim work, and home renovations, eventually launching his own business with a simple mission: deliver quality work that exceeds expectations.

Today, Garcia Carpentry serves homeowners across the Dallas metro area, from historic homes in Munger Place to modern builds in Uptown. We take pride in our reputation for reliability, attention to detail, and beautiful results. Every project is a collaboration, and we treat your home with the same respect we would our own.
