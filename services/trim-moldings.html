Trim & Moldings

Crown molding, baseboards, wainscoting, and more for a polished, refined look.

Schedule this service

Overview

Trim work is the finishing touch that elevates any room. We specialize in installing crown molding, chair rails, baseboards, and custom wainscoting with laser precision. Our experienced carpenters ensure every joint is tight and every angle is perfect, creating a seamless, high-end appearance.

What's included

  • Enhances architectural character
  • Increases home resale value
  • Hides imperfections at wall and floor junctions
  • Durable, long-lasting installation

How it works

1

Design Consultation

We review your space and recommend molding profiles and layouts that complement your home's style.

2

Precision Cutting

All pieces are measured and cut off-site or in a dedicated workspace to minimize dust and disruption.

3

Expert Installation

We install each piece with careful alignment, secure fastening, and flawless caulking and painting prep.

Common questions

Do you paint the trim after installation?

We install primed trim and can coordinate with your painter, or we can handle painting for an additional fee.

Can you install trim in a room with uneven walls?

Yes, we have techniques to accommodate uneven surfaces and ensure a clean, professional finish.
