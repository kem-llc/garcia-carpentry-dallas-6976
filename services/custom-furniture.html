Overview

Bring your dream furniture to life. Whether you need a farmhouse dining table, a minimalist desk, or a custom bed frame, we craft each piece by hand with attention to joinery and finish. We work with a variety of woods, including oak, walnut, maple, and reclaimed lumber.
